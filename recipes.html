1. Chapati with Spiced Meat

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • Water, salt, oil
  • Beef or goat meat (cut into cubes)
  • 1 onion, 2 tomatoes, 2 cloves garlic
  • Spices: black pepper, cumin, turmeric

Steps: Mix flour, water, and salt to form dough. Let it rest, then roll and cook chapatis on a hot pan with oil. For meat: Fry onions and garlic, add tomatoes and meat. Add spices, cook till tender. Serve hot with fresh chapati.

2. Pilau with Meat & Kachumbari

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice
  • Meat (beef/goat), chopped
  • 1 large onion, garlic, ginger
  • Pilau masala, salt, oil
  • Kachumbari: chopped onions, tomato, coriander, lemon juice

3. Rice with Rich Curry

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups white rice
  • Chicken or beef (boneless)
  • Onion, garlic, tomato
  • Curry powder, paprika, coconut milk (optional)

Steps: Cook rice separately. For curry, fry onions and garlic, add meat and tomatoes, cook with curry powder. Pour in coconut milk or water and simmer until thick. Serve curry over hot rice.

4. Penne Pasta with Fresh Coriander

Ingredients:

  • Penne pasta
  • Onion, garlic, tomato puree
  • Salt, black pepper, paprika
  • Fresh coriander (dhania)

Steps: Boil pasta till soft and drain. In a pan, fry onions and garlic, add tomato puree and spices. Toss in pasta and stir well. Sprinkle fresh coriander on top before serving.
